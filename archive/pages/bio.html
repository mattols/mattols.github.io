About me:

I am a geoscientist and educator primarily interested in mountain glaciers, snow hydrology, and climate science. I use satellite and UAV remote sensing, GIS, numerical modeling, and various field and lab equipment to model and monitor changes in the hydrosphere and cryosphere.

In my free time, I enjoy recreating in the mountains with my wife Jess and dogs, Lefty and Koda.

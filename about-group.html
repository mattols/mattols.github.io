Summer 2024 - Present

Mapping the Last ice of Utah

Dust on snow...Our main goals include:

  • Establish a continual snowpack monitoring program for the central Wasatch
  • Monitor seasonal dust on snow
  • Assess current snow models for predicting the onset of melt

Collaborations

A key component of this project is ongoing collaboration with Sundance Ski Resort and graduate students at the University of Utah.
